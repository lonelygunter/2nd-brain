- ***@ [IPv6]:***
> indirizzo IP a ==128 bit== (**16 bytes**) con notazioni:
> - ==full notation==: $$\text{E3D7:0000:0000:0000:51F4:9BC8:C0A8:6420}$$
> - ==shorthand notation==: $$\text{E3D7::51F4:9BC8:C0A8:6420}$$
> - ==IPv4-mapped IPv6==: $$\text{::ffff:192.0.2.128}$$
